27rroses

For years on, I have attempted to begin numerous creative pursuits, ambitious to become more of the ones I look up to the most. Exhausted by my own complacency, when am I finally going to be what I tell you I am? I won't even tell you I am anything because maybe I'm too ashamed to be proud of the little I have to show. At least I have something right? When am I going to have done enough to believe it? For all I preach, it is better to have something deemed horrible than nothing at all, why not for me too? In the most recent times of my life, I stopped doing nothing. Finally after telling myself I would just wait for a better time, I am at the better time. Actually, there was no "better time," but we all know that theres no better than know. Start living by this philosophy and make your life fun because what is it if not? I won't be happy if I don't do what I wan't and I can't get myself to listen to what anyone says. Through my art and my pursuit, I exude passion and care. Nothing matters more to me than authenticity and truth and its imperitive I embody that through what I create. I strive to be great at what I do and nothing less. I desire to be known, I desire for what I make to be known because it cannot be unseen. My photos and art are expressions of myself. We live in the same world, but see it through my eyes. I hope I can hold your curiosty for just a minute. I hope my care for technicality doesn't go unnoticed. I hope you are forced to look deeper than just what you see, to feel what it is like to live in it.
